A calm, structured approach

The goal isn’t just better marks — it’s confidence, independence and the ability to approach learning calmly.

Personalised support that fits your child

Sessions begin with understanding your child’s strengths, challenges and mindset. Lessons are tailored to what they need most — reinforcing foundations, improving technique and building confidence.

  • Clear goals and a steady pace (no last-minute cramming)
  • Targeted practice to close gaps and secure core skills
  • Confidence-first learning that transfers into the classroom

Building confidence & reducing anxiety

Many students find exams intimidating because they haven’t been shown how to manage the process. With regular practice and reassurance, they learn to approach questions with calm and clarity.

  • Mistakes treated as learning — progress over perfection
  • Exam habits that reduce stress and improve performance
  • Parents kept informed with clear next steps

Year 6 SATs Preparation

Preparing for SATs can feel like a big milestone. Support is designed to reduce pressure, build calm exam habits, and strengthen the skills students carry into secondary school.

What we cover: English & Maths

Sessions identify gaps, develop technique and build confidence through steady practice.

  • English: reading comprehension, grammar, punctuation, spelling & expression
  • Maths: arithmetic fluency, reasoning and problem-solving step-by-step
  • Technique: pacing, checking and handling unfamiliar questions calmly

Ready for the next step

SATs mark an important transition. Lessons help bridge the gap to secondary school, ensuring strong foundations in reading, writing and maths.
